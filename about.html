Sofia Barraza is a graduating Computer Science major at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona.

Sofia has loved technology as long as she can remember. Growing up, she drove her parents crazy with the questions "why?" and "what does this button do?" She discovered her love of gaming at the age of seven with Disney's Toontown Online. Since then, she has spent countless hours playing Lord of the Rings Online, DC Universe Online, World of Warcraft, League of Legends, and more.

In college, her love of gaming led her to venture into Computer Science. She took her first coding class Spring quarter of freshman year and hasn't looked back since.
